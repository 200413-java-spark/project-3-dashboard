NY State Oil Production


"NY State Oil Production" is a project developed by Revature's April 23rd 2020 Big Data Batch. The objective is to use all the concepts learned throughout the training program to build an automated big data pipeline for streaming in oil and gas information from New York State Dept. of Environmental Conservation Division of Mineral Resources and provide a visual representation for tracking the history of a wellbore's production of oil, gas, or water during its lifecycle.
